  • Team of maximum : Individual

Event Rules

Round 1

Candidates have to submit an abstract of their research which he had done on any of the topics or themes mentioned below. NOTE: plagiarism will not be accepted so avoid copying from any of the websites. Do your own research and write on your own, take help from the internet just to collect information but ideas should be strictly yours.

Round 2

In this round presentation or your research will take place (possibly on MS teams). You have to present your work in front of judges and explain them.


Note

  • On that day only you will have to present paper based on IEEE format. The paper has to be submitted in pdf format
  • Candidate will be judged on the basis of his quality of work, presentation and way of writing his/her article in IEEE format
