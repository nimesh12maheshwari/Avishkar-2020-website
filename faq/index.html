Frequently Asked Questions

1- What is Avishkar?

Avishkar is the annual techno-management fest organized by National Institute of Technology, Allahabad (MNNIT) instituted for the enrichment of Engineering and Science with the aim of providing a platform for a multitude of students to showcase their technical ingenuity and prowess. Serving as a platform for providing an opportunity to ' Ideate, Innovate & Create ' , Avishkar is an experience, full of amusement and learning opportunities.

2- What does Avishkar include?

Avishkar includes:
Innovative events to test the technical and general knowhow of the participant. Inceptive initiatives to make society a better place to live in. Inspiring speakers to know about the eminent personalities and their life stories. Illustrative exhibits to update the participant about the ever ending development taking place in the engineering industry.

3- When and where is Avishkar going to be scheduled?

Avishkar'20 will be a grandiose 4-days festival to be held from 31st October to 3rd November, 2020 in online mode amidst the Covid19 outbreak.

4- How can a participant register for Avishkar?

All the interested participants can register themselves via the register option available at the home page. The registration charges are Rs.350 per student for all the events of Avishkar 2020 except Gnosiomania.

5- What is the registration fee for Gnosiomania and Gnotalks?

The participation fee is Rs.250 for Gnotalks + Gnosiomania quizzes and Rs.200 for only the Gnosiomania quizzes.

6- How will a participant pay the registration fee?

The participants will be contacted via mail with the payment link attached to it. All the participants are requested to pay the fee within 48 hours of receiving the mail.

7- How can a participant register for a team event?

All the interested participants can register themselves by following the steps mentioned:
  • Login to your avishkar account and lock your profile after updating the details.
  • Head to the manage teams sub-section and create a team.
  • Send invites to the respective team members.
  • Wait for the team members to accept the invitation.
  • Register the team for the particular event.

8- How can a participant register for an individual event?

All the interested participants can register themselves by following the steps mentioned:
  • Login to your avishkar account and lock your profile after updating the details.
  • Head to the manage teams sub-section and create a team.
  • Register the team for the particular event.
