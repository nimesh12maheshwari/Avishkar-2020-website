Frequently Asked Questions
What is the participation Fee for Avishkar 2019?

The participation fee is 600 per person. This will include being able to participate in any event of Avishkar 2k19, as well as attending any event during the Fest. The accomodation will be charged extra.

How can I get to the venue?

The map and Contact details are listed within the contact information.You will also receive a confirmation email with location map.

What about accommodation?

Accomodation for outside participants will be provided in hostel and Student Activity Centre (SAC). There is seprate accomodation for girls in the EDC Guest House.

What is the participation Fee for Gnosiomania?

The participation fee is 150 per person without accomodation and 300 with accomodation.
